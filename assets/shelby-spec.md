# Canonical Shelby Specification (v1.1)

Authoritative reference: **Magnifier Shelby** (wooden, rounded, dimensional)

**v0.1.0 Celebration Shelby**

This version refines curvature, shading, and bottom edge behavior while preserving all core traits.

## 1. Overall Silhouette & Proportions

### Body Shape
- Wide rectangular block
- Width ≈ 1.4–1.6× height
- Compact, grounded feel
- Low center of gravity

### Corners
- Generously rounded
- Soft pill-like curvature
- No sharp rectangle corners

### Outline
- Thick dark outline
- Consistent weight around silhouette
- Slightly heavier on outer contour than interior lines

## 2. Body Material & Wood Treatment

### Material
- Warm golden wood
- Vertical wood grain lines
- Slight plank segmentation shading

### Shading Model
- Clear highlight zone near upper third
- Gentle midtone across center
- Soft shadow pooling near bottom curvature
- No painterly texture
- Vector-clean but dimensional

### Critical Rules
- No horizontal seam across middle
- No waist line
- No separate base element

## 3. Bottom Edge (Clarified)

### Structure
- Bottom edge is wood
- No black belt
- No color-separated band
- No flat stripe

### Form
- Bottom corners rounded and volumetric
- Shading wraps under body
- Darkening is gradient-based, not a stripe
- Legs attach directly into wood body

**This is curvature shading — not a band.**

## 4. Chest Shelf Opening

### Placement
- Horizontal cutout in upper third of body

### Interior
- Darker brown than outer wood
- Slight inset shading for depth

### Books
- Bright colors: blue, red, yellow, green
- Simple rectangular shapes
- Minimal detail
- Slight tilt variation allowed
- Sit flush within opening
- Never wrapped
- Never overly textured

## 5. Face Placement (Non-Negotiable)

### Position
- Located in lower half of wooden body
- Entirely below shelf opening

### Eyes
- Large glossy black circles
- High reflection shine
- Slightly wide spacing
- Not touching shelf opening
- Float directly on wood

### Mouth
- Small curved smile
- Centered between eyes
- Slightly above bottom edge
- No heavy outline around mouth cavity

### Cheeks
- Circular
- Symmetrical
- Soft red/orange
- Slight transparency feel

**No framing. No inset panel. Face lives directly on wood surface.**

## 6. Head / Terminal Cap

### Form
- Dark charcoal/navy
- Slight forward lip
- Soft rectangular form
- Subtle highlight edge

### Icon
- Green `>` arrow
- Small horizontal dash beside it
- Clean, simple
- No exaggerated distortion

**Cap must not overpower body proportions.**
**Cap is not actually a hat, it is a terminal emerging from the head of the bookshelf**

## 7. Limbs

### Arms
- Rounded cylindrical
- Same warm yellow tone
- Simple mitten hands
- No complex finger anatomy

### Legs
- Short
- Chunky
- Slight outward stance
- Dark rounded shoes
- No heavy platform base

## 8. Shadow
- Small soft oval beneath
- Light opacity
- No pedestal
- No environment unless requested
- Alpha background preferred

## Explicit Non-Canonical Traits

- No black base band
- No belt-like stripe
- No waist seam
- No extra rim shading around bottom
- No hyper-3D realism
- No textured wood carving
- No painterly brush strokes
- No heavy floor stage
- No exaggerated 3D perspective distortion

## Style Language

- High saturation
- Thick clean outlines
- Soft dimensional shading
- Cartoon vector-ish
- Compact silhouette
- Expressive but simple

## Form Personality Notes

Shelby should feel:
- Solid
- Compact
- Slightly squishy
- Warm
- Friendly
- Not tall or lanky
- Not top-heavy

**He is a bookshelf wearing a terminal cap — not a robot wearing wood.**
